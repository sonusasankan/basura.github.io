Our Working Process

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving Your Business Goals

During the initial consultation, we analyze your current waste management system and the scale of the services required for your specific business or property. This allows us to understand your needs and create an optimal solution.

We conduct thorough research on your specific market which includes site visits, regulatory analysis, and internal review of waste policies.

Our highly trained Material Conservationist are briefed on your specific requirements while we integrate Basura’s data collection systems with the your existing infrastructure. Additionally, we work side by side with private couriers, municipalities, and processors to insure waste is properly collected.

We will complete a full implementation of Basura's waste management system across the your facilities. Material Conservationists weigh and record data on waste streams, providing detailed reports and insights. Lastly, Basura will provide continuous support, including regular check-ins, troubleshooting, and updates on best practices.

Basura conducts educational sessions and workshops to inform employees, residents, or tenants about waste management recommendations, the importance of recycling, and how to reduce waste generation.

Basura provides regular reports on waste segregation like recycling assessments, diversion rates and other sustainability met. This includes integrating waste management data into their Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) reports, helping them to demonstrate their commitment to sustainability and social responsibility to stakeholders. We also work with our cleints to analyze data and identify opportunities for further waste reduction and sustainability improvements.

We will have a continuous feedback loop between you and Basura to ensure goals are met and services are optimized. Regular reviews to assess the effectiveness of the waste management system and discuss any changes or enhancements.
