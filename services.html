Services

On-Site Waste Sorting :

  • Material Conservationists: Our trained experts manually sort your waste to maximize recycling and composting efforts.
  • Customized Sorting Plans: Tailored solutions to fit the specific needs of your properties and business.

Data Collection and Analysis :

  • Waste Profiling: Detailed tracking of different waste streams by weight, providing valuable insights into your waste generation patterns.
  • Reporting and Analytics: Comprehensive reports and analytics to help you understand and improve your waste management practices.
  • AI Integration: Utilizing artificial intelligence to enhance data analysis and provide actionable recommendations for waste reduction and sustainability.

Sustainable Solutions for Enterprises :

  • Corporate Partnerships: Working with large businesses and publicly traded companies to integrate Basura’s system into their operations.
  • Tailored ESG Strategies: Developing custom plans to meet your specific Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) objectives.
  • Sustainability Reporting: Assisting with the creation of detailed sustainability reports for stakeholders and investors

Waste Management :

  • Paper and Cardboard Recycling: Efficient collection and processing of paper products.
  • Plastic, Glass, and Metal Recycling: Comprehensive recycling solutions for a variety of materials.
  • Organic Waste Composting: Transforming food scraps, yard waste, and compostable paper into valuable compost.
  • Textile Recycling: Solutions for recycling clothing, linens, shoes, and accessories.
  • Electronic Waste Disposal: Safe and compliant disposal of electronics and hazardous household products.
  • Special Waste Handling: Management of construction and demolition debris, furniture, and other difficult-to-recycle items.

Programming and Workshops :

  • Employee Training: Educating your staff on best practices for waste reduction and recycling.
  • Community Outreach: Programs to engage and educate the local community on sustainable waste management practices.

Waste Audits and Assessments :

  • nitial Waste Audit: We conduct a thorough assessment of your current waste management practices to identify areas for improvement.
  • Ongoing Assessments: Regular evaluations to ensure continuous improvement and alignment with sustainability goals.
